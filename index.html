Compilation of tweaks that I think personally improve things as they are in the vanilla game. The vast majority of the tweaks are designed to be simple improvements of underpowered items or abilities. Some of them overhaul the game a bit more extensively, though not radically.
Latest Release
This mod overhauls the Shadowdancer kit added by BeamDog, deeply overhauling things like Hide in Plain Sight, Shadowstep, as well as adding new and interesting abilities that make the kit more balanced and in various ways, also more powerful and viable as a single class.
Latest Release
Adds a variety of weapon oils that grant elemental damage, poison effects, and more into the game. Mostly to stores, but some can be found as loot from enemies. They're designed to be pretty mild and on the somewhat expensive side, for balance reasons.
Latest Release
Introduces changes to what creatures can be backstabbed, and also who gets to see through invisibility, which is sometimes relevant. The goal is to make the life of thieves, especially in ToB, more rewarding.
